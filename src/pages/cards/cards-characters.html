Character sizing is controlled using a character count class. Characters are centered by setting "display: flex;" on the parent in CSS, while "align-center" and "align-middle" classes are added to the parent in Markup. Card style is based Material Desing CSS. Needs fine tuning to cover all screen-sizes.' ---
